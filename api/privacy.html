LEGAL DOCUMENT — RMApp APP

Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: APRIL 14, 2026 · RMAPP

This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use any RMApp Radio mobile application, which allows users to listen to live radio streams and webradios. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. This policy is an integral part of our Terms of Use Copyright & DMCA.

This policy is also an agreement between RMApp ("RadioFM" / "we" / "us") and the user. RMApp has not concealed anything about its platform model from Broadcasters, Podcasters, or Users.

§ 1

About the Service

RMApp apps are radio directories and aggregators. RMApp provides a platform to Radio Broadcasters and Podcasters to index their Radio Stations across all RM products and devices. The App does not host, store, copy, or retransmit any audio content. All radio streams are publicly available sources provided by the respective broadcasters on their own servers. No audio data passes through RMApp servers at any point.

The App functions as a specialized technical player (User-Agent) that facilitates a direct connection between the user's device and the broadcaster's public URL. The App does not modify, alter, or insert any additional audio into the streams. The Service acts as a passive pass-through directory.

§ 2

Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect minimal personal and usage data to improve your experience, display relevant ads, and analyze usage patterns. We do not collect names, email addresses, or any directly identifying personal information unless you voluntarily contact us.

2.2 Non-Personal Information

We may collect non-personal data automatically, including:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system and version
  • Country or language settings
  • IP address (for analytics and regional content)
  • App interactions and usage data
  • Session duration and crash logs

2.3 Location Data

The App may request access to your approximate location to serve region-based radio streams. This is optional and can be denied at any time. Precise location data is never collected.

2.4 Listening Data

We do not collect or store your individual listening history linked to any personal identifier. Usage data related to station popularity may be collected in aggregated and anonymized form only.

2.5 Camera and Gallery Permission

We may request access to your camera and gallery to allow you to take or select images for updating your profile photo or uploading station logos for our search feature.

§ 3

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services for advertising, analytics, and improving app functionality. These third parties may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

3.1 Advertising (AdMob, Meta, AppLovin)

We use Google AdMob, AppLovin, and Meta to show personalized or non-personalized ads. They may collect the Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS), device and usage data, and approximate location if enabled. Users in the EU/EEA are prompted for consent under GDPR and may opt in or out at any time.

3.2 Analytics

We may use Google Analytics for Firebase to monitor app performance and Crashlytics to track stability. These services collect anonymized data only.

3.3 Radio Stream Providers

When you play a station, your device connects directly to the broadcaster's server or their CDN. The broadcaster may collect connection data (IP address) per their own privacy policy. RMApp has no control over broadcaster data practices. The App acts as a passive pass-through player.

§ 4

Consent & User Choices

4.1 App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

For iOS 14.5 and above, we request your permission before tracking activity across other apps and websites, per Apple's ATT framework.

4.2 Personalized Ads

You can manage advertising preferences from the "Privacy Settings" screen in the App at any time.

4.3 Opt-Out

You can restrict or opt out of data collection via iOS Privacy Settings or the app-specific privacy settings within the App.

§ 5

Data Storage and Security

  • All collected data is anonymized and encrypted in transit
  • We retain user data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy
  • No audio content, no sensitive personal data, and no listening history linked to individuals is stored on our servers
  • We do not sell personal data to third parties
§ 6

Broadcaster Rights & Content Removal

All radio streams, logos, and station names are the intellectual property of their respective owners. RMApp does not claim ownership of any content.

If you are a broadcaster and wish to have your station removed or updated, contact us at contact.rmapp@gmail.com or use our Broadcaster Portal. All valid removal requests are processed within 24 hours.

§ 7

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we discover such data has been collected, it will be deleted immediately.

§ 8

International Users — GDPR & CCPA

8.1 European Users (GDPR)

If you access the App from the EU or EEA, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, or object to processing of your data, and to withdraw consent at any time. Contact us at contact.rmapp@gmail.com.

8.2 California Users (CCPA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, whether it is sold (we do not sell it), request deletion, and not be discriminated against for exercising these rights. Contact us at contact.rmapp@gmail.com.

§ 9

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via in-app alert or App Store update notes. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
