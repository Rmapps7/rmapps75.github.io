§ 1

The Service

Radios France and all RM Radio apps are the digital equivalent of a radio receiver. The Service consists of an index of radio stations designed to simplify finding radio programming from broadcast or radio sources, whether available through any software applications made available for mobile devices or computer systems.

RM App is not a broadcaster and its directory service just redirects users to specific content providers in accordance with their publicly available services. RM App is an intermediary which connects (via embedded hyperlinks) users to radios broadcasting over the Internet. RM App does not own any of the brands it lists or claim any rights in the content that listeners may access.

RM App does not host, store, copy, or retransmit any audio content. The App functions as a specialized technical player (User-Agent) that facilitates a direct connection between the user's device and the broadcaster's public URL.

The RM Radio API operates as a radio station directory and hyperlink service. All stream URLs point directly to the broadcasters' own servers or their authorized CDN infrastructure.
