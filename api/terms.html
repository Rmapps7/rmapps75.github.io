Broadcaster Rights, Consent & Station Indexing

The RMApp Radio platform provides broadcasters with full control over their presence in the directory. By submitting a station to the RMApp Radio directory, a broadcaster grants RMApp a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to list and link their publicly available streams within the RMApp Radio platform and associated mobile applications.

RM App has not concealed anything about its platform model from Broadcasters, Podcasters, or Users. The platform's directory model, indexing process, and takedown procedures are fully documented and publicly accessible.

Any broadcaster whose station appears in the RMApp Radio directory may at any time:

  • Request immediate removal of their station — processed within 24 hours, no questions asked
  • Claim and verify ownership of their station via the Broadcaster Portal
  • Update their station information, stream URL, logo, or genre
  • Opt their station into or out of specific categories or regional apps

Takedown requests are processed within 24 hours of receipt, via the Broadcaster Portal or by emailing contact.rmapp@gmail.com. Upon a valid request, the station is immediately removed from all API responses and RMApp Radio apps.

Official Public Broadcaster APIs

The following stations are sourced from official public broadcaster APIs, which are publicly documented and freely accessible:

  • Radio France (France) — official streams via icecast.radiofrance.fr, a public broadcaster funded by the French government
  • SRG SSR (Switzerland) — official API via developer.srgssr.ch, Switzerland's national public broadcaster
  • ARD (Germany) — official API via api.ardmediathek.de, Germany's public broadcasting network
  • NHK World (Japan) — official stream via nhkworld.jp, Japan's public international broadcaster
