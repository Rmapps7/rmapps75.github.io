LEGAL DOCUMENT — RM RADIO APP

Privacy Policy

LAST UPDATED: APRIL 5, 2026 · RM APP · SEBASTIEN ROUSSEAU

This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and shared when you use any RM Radio mobile application, which allows users to listen to live radio streams and webradios.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. This policy is an integral part of our Terms of Use.

§ 1

About the Service

RM Radio apps are radio directories and aggregators. The App does not host, store, copy, or retransmit any audio content. All radio streams are publicly available sources provided by the respective broadcasters on their own servers or authorized CDN infrastructure. RM Radio acts solely as an intermediary that connects users to publicly available streams. No audio data passes through our servers at any point.

§ 2

Information We Collect

2.1. Personal Information

We collect minimal personal and usage data to improve your experience, display relevant ads, and analyze usage patterns. We do not collect names, email addresses, or any directly identifying personal information unless you voluntarily contact us.

2.2. Non-Personal Information

We may collect non-personal data automatically, including:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system and version
  • Country or language settings
  • IP address (for analytics and regional content)
  • App interactions and usage data
  • Session duration and crash logs

2.3. Location Data

The App may request access to your approximate location to serve region-based radio streams. This is optional and can be denied at any time. Precise location data is never collected.

2.4. Listening Data

We do not collect or store your individual listening history linked to any personal identifier. Usage data related to station popularity may be collected in aggregated and anonymized form only.

§ 3

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services for advertising, analytics, and improving our app's functionality. These third parties may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party services.

3.1. Advertising (AdMob, Meta, AppLovin)

We use Google AdMob, AppLovin, and Meta to show personalized or non-personalized ads. They may collect:

  • Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS)
  • Device and usage data
  • Approximate location (if enabled)

Users in the EU and EEA are prompted for consent under GDPR and may opt in or opt out of personalized ads at any time.

3.2. Analytics Tools

We may use Google Analytics for Firebase to monitor app performance and usage, and Crashlytics to track app crashes and stability. These services collect anonymized data that help us improve the app experience.

3.3. Radio Stream Providers

When you play a radio station, your device connects directly to the broadcaster's server or their authorized CDN. The broadcaster may collect data about your connection (such as IP address) in accordance with their own privacy policy. RM Radio has no control over and is not responsible for the data practices of individual radio stations or their CDN providers.

The App does not modify, alter, or edit the audio content provided by broadcasters. We do not insert additional audio advertisements into the streams. The App acts as a passive pass-through player.

§ 4

Consent & User Choices

4.1. App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

For users on iOS 14.5 and above, we request your permission before tracking activity across other apps and websites, in accordance with Apple's ATT framework.

4.2. Consent for Personalized Ads

You can manage your advertising preferences from the "Privacy Settings" screen in the App at any time.

4.3. Opt-Out of Data Collection

You can restrict or opt out of data collection via your device settings (iOS Privacy settings) or the app-specific privacy settings within the App.

§ 5

Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information:

  • All collected data is anonymized and encrypted in transit
  • We retain user data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy
  • No audio content, no sensitive personal data, and no listening history linked to individual users is stored on our servers
  • We do not sell personal data to third parties
§ 6

Third-Party Content and Broadcaster Rights

All radio streams, logos, station names, and related media accessible through the App are the intellectual property of their respective radio station owners, broadcasters, or licensors. RM Radio does not claim any ownership of this content.

If you are a broadcaster or rights holder and believe your content should not be included in our directory, or you wish to update how your station is displayed, please contact us at contact.rmapp@gmail.com. We will process all valid removal requests within 24 hours.

§ 7

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or 16 in certain jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If we discover that personal data from a child has been collected, it will be deleted immediately.

§ 8

International Users — GDPR & CCPA Compliance

8.1. European Users (GDPR)

If you are accessing the App from the European Union or European Economic Area, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time

8.2. California Users (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected about you
  • Know whether your personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising these rights

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact.rmapp@gmail.com.
